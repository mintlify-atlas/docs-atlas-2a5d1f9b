title: Custom Abilities
description: Redux-exclusive abilities and the imported ability packs available in Legends of Dota Redux.
---

Alongside every standard Dota 2 ability, Legends of Dota Redux ships a large library of custom and imported abilities. These are defined in `npc_abilities_custom.txt` and implemented in the `vscripts/abilities/` directory.
